About Me

Michael was born in Kenosha, Wisconsin (where he has lived for his whole life) on November 1st, 1999. He lives at home with his parents, Steven and Brenda Christman, and younger brother, Sean Christman. Michael and Sean are both currently attending Bellevue University (in their third year of college). Michael is pursuing his bachelor's degree in web development. while his brother Sean is pursuing a degree in software development.

Michael is an aspiring front-end web developer with 4-5 years of experience building dynamic, functional, and responsive websites. While his list of projects on his resume is still relatively limited, he strives to deliver web applications which fulfill or even surpass clients' expectations.

He is proficient in several programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Java, and Python. He also possesses an intermediate level of knowledge with SQL databases and ASP.Net applications. His problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and interpersonal communication are qualities that should make him a valuable asset to a professional web development team.

One of Michael's greatest strengths is ability to collaborate with clients, project managers, and web developers in his group to ensure strict adherence to project completion deadlines and user demands are being met. He is an effective, empathetic communicator, always inquiring and considering the client's desires and preferences as a top priority.

Michael has been diagnosed on the autism spectrum at a very young age. So in addition to his web designer qualifications, Michael's neurodivergence is what gives him a different perspective on various facets of life. He is a creative and unique thinker who is always looking for fresh and innovative ideas to address web development challenges. He is dedicated on staying posted on the most recent trends, events, and advancements in the I.T. industry. Michael's autism are what inspired his interests and passions.

He is a self-proclaimed cinephile who loves watching and reviewing movies and television series. Michael's additional hobbies include playing video games, reading, and creating personal websites. Overall, Michael is a skilled web developer who is devoted to providing satisfactory results. His aspirations and ambition combined with his technical knowledge, creative mind, and enthusiasm to learn and grow with peers make him an ideal team player for collaborative web projects.
